My Movies

The Sixth Sense

Child psychologist, Malcom Crowe is one night visited by an ex-patient named Vincent Grey who is enraged. He wounds Crowe, then kills himself. A few months later Crowe is visited by a 9-year old boy named Cole. He sees dead people. Crowe, at first thinks he is seeing things, but after spending a lot of time with Cole, he discovers he may be seeing dead people after all.


Director  

M. Night Shyamalan


Main actors

Bruce Willis, Haley Joel Osment, Toni Collette
